Our Mission

Our mission at Joint Wallet is to empower communities through transparent, efficient group financial management and lending. We foster accountability, provide tools for collective saving, and promote leadership and financial literacy for economic well-being.

Our Plan

To raise a minimum amount of ksh. 200,000 by the end of 2024 achieved through our monthly savings that we are conducting monthly. This will help raise our loan financial security as a group, we are currently 2/5% to hitting the target

Our Vision

Joint Wallet envisions a future where communities thrive financially through transparency, efficiency, and collective empowerment. We equip members with tools and support for confident financial navigation, fostering a brighter, more secure future
